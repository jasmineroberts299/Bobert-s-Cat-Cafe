Since the creation of Bobert's Cat Cafe, the restaurant has been booming and the love of cats has skyrocketed! Marjorine Bobert wouldn't believe her eyes today that her little shop became a great business and is succeeded by one of her own relatives. The orginial idea came from Marjorine's aunt, Creame, who was also an avid cat lover. She loved to come visit Marjorine and feed her cats bits of bread and coffee.
